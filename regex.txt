There were 3 types of grep:
grep - original
egrep - Extended grep which uses Extended Regex
fgrep - fast grep
The three versions were merged together in 1992.

Classes:
[:alnum:] [:alpha:] [:lower:] ...

Match any char. within the given range:
[a-z] [0-9] [A-Z] ...

Match any char. NOT within the given range:
[!a-z] [!0-9] [!A-Z] ...

Collating (Multi-Char.) elements:
[.ch.] [.sh.] [.sch.] ...
