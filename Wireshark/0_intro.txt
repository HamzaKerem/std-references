PCAPNG files, or "Packet Capture Next Generation" files, are a format used to
store network packet data. They are an evolution of the older PCAP format and
provide several enhancements, including support for more advanced capture
options, the ability to store more detailed information about packets, and the
ability to include multiple types of packet data in a single file.
